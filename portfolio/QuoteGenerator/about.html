About - Quote Generator

Quote Generator is a random quotation generator which randomly generates piece of quote from 3 sentence fragments. The quotes are generated randomly one by one. First choose between 2 types of quote generators then select the number of quotes generated (between 1 and 5) form the drop-down list respectively, then click on the "Generate Quote" button to see your generated quote.
